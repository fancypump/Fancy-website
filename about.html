About Fancy Pump

Your trusted partner in water pump manufacturing since 2008 — from Wenling, China to 60+ countries worldwide.

Company Profile

Specialized Manufacturer of Premium Water Pumps

Fancy Pump (Zhejiang) Co., Ltd is a specialized manufacturer and supplier of high-quality water pumps, based in Wenling City, Zhejiang Province, China — the heart of the country's pump manufacturing industry.

We focus on the production and sale of various water pumps and spare parts, covering household use, building water supply, garden irrigation, HVAC, fire fighting, sewage treatment, and industrial applications. Our portfolio spans nearly 60 product series with more than 10,000 specifications.

With business relationships established in over 60 countries and regions, and subsidiaries in Saudi Arabia, Russia, Uzbekistan, Algeria, Turkey, and Bangladesh, we serve world-famous pump manufacturers and wholesalers globally.

Manufacturing Strength

Strong Processing Capabilities

Fancy Pump operates a modern production facility equipped with advanced machining centers, automated assembly lines, and precision testing equipment. Our manufacturing capabilities ensure consistent quality across all product lines.

With years of experience in pump manufacturing, we have built a robust production system that can handle everything from raw material processing to final assembly and testing — all under one roof.
